About Us

China Bangladesh Students Society is a Non-Political, Non-Profitable, Non-Government, Voluntary and Educational Organization. It was established on April 25, 2021 in Bogura district, the northwestern part of Bangladesh. Our vision is to provide proper guidelines to the students belongs to Bogura District as well as every districtâ€™s students of Bangladesh who wish to go for higher education in China to establish cordial relations with Bangladeshi students/worker residing in China, to co-operate with them in all possible ways And research opportunities & facilities in China.We provide proper guidelines to poor and meritorious students, provide financial assistance when needed to inspire everyone about the culture and environment of education and research in China. We also provide necessary advice and support to the members of the Society to be self-reliant to increase public awareness of a society free from social degradation in the area. We also motive the members of the society in practice of healthy application, sports and culture to provide formal and non-formal education in both rural and urban area to provide & support mother and child health care, nutrition, blood donation program, eye checkup and treatment, Vaccination, for the poor.
